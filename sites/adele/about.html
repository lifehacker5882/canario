Adele Laurie Blue Adkins is a globally recognized English singer-songwriter famous for her powerful, soulful voice and emotional ballads. She achieved colossal success with her second album, 21 (2011), featuring major hits like "Rolling in the Deep" and "Someone Like You." Her records, including 25 and 30, have consistently broken sales records, establishing her as one of the best-selling music artists of all time and a multiple Grammy Award winner.
