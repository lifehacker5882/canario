Marshall Bruce Mathers III, a world famous rapper born on October 17, 1972, in St. Joseph, Missouri, USA. He is know by his stage name Eminem and is often cited as one of the greatest rapper in modern times. Eminem gained major popularity with his album "The Slim Shady" released in 1999, which won him his first Grammy Award.
